Chat with an Arts and Science Student

Connect with our student ambassadors to learn more about programs, classes and campus life. 

Follow the steps below to get started and don’t hesitate to ask anything that can help you make the best decision for you:

 STEP 1 : Select the student ambassador you wish to connect with.
 STEP 2 : Create an Ambassador Platform account.
 STEP 3 : Start chatting!

While our ambassadors are happy to chat about their ArtSci experience, they cannot answer questions related to application processing or study permits. Instead, please contact the  Arts and Science Recruitment Team. For immigration matters, please contact the  International Student and Study Abroad Centre.
